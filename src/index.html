The #NO2Tubes-300Campaign is a (for now) one shot project of the global open environmental data platform Sensor.Community.

Between the 1st of October and the 31st of October 2020, 300 diffusion tubes for NO2 were installed in 9 cities in order to measure the average daily concentration of this gas in the air.
